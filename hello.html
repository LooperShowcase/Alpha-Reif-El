All About Fish

Introduction to Fish

Fish are aquatic animals that are typically covered in scales and breathe through gills. They are an essential part of many ecosystems, providing food, oxygen, and maintaining biodiversity in aquatic environments.

Types of Fish

  • Freshwater Fish: These fish live in rivers, lakes, and ponds. Examples include salmon, bass, and trout.
  • Saltwater Fish: These fish live in the ocean. Examples include tuna, cod, and clownfish.
  • Brackish Fish: These fish live in areas where freshwater meets saltwater, like estuaries. Examples include tilapia and some species of mullet.

Beautiful Fish

Why Are Fish Important?

Fish play a crucial role in ecosystems. They help maintain a balance in food webs, provide oxygen, and contribute to the health of aquatic plants and coral reefs. Many fish species are also vital for the global economy, supporting industries such as fishing and aquaculture.
